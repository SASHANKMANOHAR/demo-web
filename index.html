About Mana Orru Ruchulu

Our journey in bringing authentic Telugu cuisine to you

Our Story

Mana Orru Ruchulu, which means "Our Village Flavors" in Telugu, was founded in 2010 by the Reddy family with a vision to bring the authentic tastes of Andhra and Telangana cuisine to food enthusiasts.

What started as a small family kitchen has now grown into a beloved culinary destination, preserving traditional recipes passed down through generations while incorporating contemporary cooking techniques.

Our Philosophy

We believe that food is not just about taste; it's about creating memories and experiences. At Mana Orru Ruchulu, we are committed to:

  • Using authentic spices and ingredients sourced from regional farmers
  • Preparing each dish with traditional methods to preserve authenticity
  • Creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere that celebrates Telugu culture
  • Offering exceptional service that makes every guest feel like family

Our Team

Our culinary team is led by Head Chef Venkat Reddy, who brings over 25 years of experience in traditional Telugu cuisine. Together with our dedicated staff, we work to create an unforgettable dining experience for every guest.
